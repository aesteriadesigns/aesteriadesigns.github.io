01.About

Hi! My name is Kayla and I am a freelance graphic designer and front-end web developer.

I started teaching myself how to code in 2011 when I was 15 years old. I quickly became enamoured by it and as a result I started making Tumblr themes over at Peach Themes has a following of 2300+ people. I provide both free and premium themes as well custom commissions for clients.

I have a Diploma of Web Development from 2016 and I am currently studying a Diploma of Graphic Design set to graduate at the end of 2018.

In my spare time I like to spend countless hours creating new D&D characters I'll probably never use, reading (and on the rare occasion, writing) poetry, immersing myself into fictional universes, and drinking copious amounts of bubble tea.
